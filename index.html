Are you looking for a job or do you feel it is time to take a new step in your career? Come to one of CareerMatch's fairs to find the perfect career match and end up with a company that suits you.

Today we spend our time on our jobs so much that it is important to have a job that you really enjoy. In addition to enjoying the work, it is important to have good working conditions, a good salary, a good working environment and colleagues. The world has developed so much, which has also contributed to us having endless career professions to choose from.
Take the step today and meet your potential employer at one of CareerMatch's fairs. We at CareerMatch arrange job fairs throughout Sweden and offer the opportunity to introduce you to different employers and get to know the companies better. So bring your CV, prepare questions you want to ask your future employer and give everything you have during the speed interviews that will take place. Maybe you will find the dream job that has not been applied for because of the convenience.

Most common work industries at our fairs:

  • The customer service industry
  • The beauty industry
  • Various office industries
  • The health industry
  • The developer industry
  • The healthcare industry

What we can offer at our fairs:

  • Meet over 50 potential employers
  • Speed interviews with different companies
  • The chance to upgrade your working career
  • The chance to change your career direction
  • Help you upgrade your resume
  • Help you prepare for the interviews
